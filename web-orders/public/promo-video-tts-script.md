Welcome to ReelAgad. Here is how to create your reel in just a few simple steps.

Start by going to the order page. In the Upload section, choose what you want to use as your background. You can upload your own video, select from available clips, or choose none if you only want caption style output. If you use a clip, pick your sound mode: narrator only, clip sound only, or clip sound plus narrator.

Next, manage your reel details. Choose your font, output screen size, caption position, and caption style so the video matches your preferred look. Then add your script and optional title. If your uploaded video has speech, ReelAgad can detect transcript and help you keep your script length aligned with video duration. After that, choose your narrator voice. You can also add optional customer details like name, email, and address for easier record keeping. As you update your settings, use the live preview to check layout and estimated price. When everything looks good, continue to payment. You can choose standard checkout or QR payment when available.

After successful payment, you are redirected to the Order Receipt page. Here you can see your order reference, date, status, selected reel settings, and payment details. Save this page for your records and future support.

Once processing is complete, your downloadable files appear on the receipt page. Your main output is the video file, ready for posting or sharing. You also get an SRT subtitle file, which contains timed captions you can reuse on other platforms. You also receive the transcribed text file of the video content for documentation, repurposing, or content editing.

These outputs make it easy to publish faster across different channels. Use the video for social posting, the SRT for accessibility and subtitle workflows, and the text transcript for captions, descriptions, blogs, or scripts.

If your order has multiple reels, download each set one by one. Keep your order reference and receipt link so you can return anytime and retrieve your files.

With ReelAgad, the full workflow is simple: upload, customize, pay, and download production ready assets in one place.
